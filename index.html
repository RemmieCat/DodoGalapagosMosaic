Objective

Score the most points by matching tile patterns to cards in your hand.

Turn Actions

  • Shift: Discard 1 card to swap two adjacent tiles (no diagonals).
  • Flip: Discard 1 card to flip a tile to its opposite side.
  • Score: Play a card whose pattern matches the current board.
  • Skip: Draw 2 cards (max 6). Ends your turn. Cannot skip two turns in a row.

End of Turn

Draw back up to 4 cards. Pass the device to the next player.

Winning

Endgame triggers when any player reaches the card threshold. All players finish the round, then scores are tallied. Bonus: most 1-pt cards = +3 pts.

Card Thresholds

  • 2 players — 10 cards
  • 3 players — 9 cards
  • 4 players — 8 cards
  • 5 players — 7 cards

Setup

16-card mini-deck: 8×1pt, 4×2pt, 3×3pt, 1×5pt. Draw 4 to start.

Strike Mechanic

You must score at least one card per turn. Failing earns a strike.

Actions

  • Shift / Flip / Score: Same as multiplayer.
  • Skip: Draw 2 cards — only if first action, ≤4 cards in hand, didn't skip last turn, and cards remain.

Winning & Losing

  • 🏆 Win: Score all 16 cards.
  • 💀 Lose: Accumulate 4 strikes.
