Objective

Score the most points by matching tile patterns to cards in your hand.

Turn Actions

  • Shift: Discard 1 card to swap two adjacent tiles (no diagonals).
  • Flip: Discard 1 card to flip a tile to its opposite side.
  • Score: Play a card whose pattern matches the current board. Collect its points.
  • Skip: Draw 2 cards (max 6 in hand). Ends your turn immediately. Cannot skip two turns in a row.

End of Turn

Draw back up to 4 cards. Pass the device to the next player.

Winning

Endgame triggers when any player collects enough cards. Each remaining player gets one more turn, then scores are tallied.

Bonus: Player(s) with the most 1-point cards earn +3 points.

Card Thresholds to Trigger Endgame

  • 2 players — 10 cards
  • 3 players — 9 cards
  • 4 players — 8 cards
  • 5 players — 7 cards

Setup

Use a custom 16-card mini-deck: 8×1pt, 4×2pt, 3×3pt, 1×5pt. Draw 4 cards to start.

Strike Mechanic

You must score at least one card each turn. If you end your turn without scoring, you gain 1 strike.

Actions

  • Shift / Flip / Score: Same rules as multiplayer.
  • Skip: Draw 2 cards. Only allowed if it's your first action, you have ≤ 4 cards, you didn't skip last turn, and cards are available.

Winning & Losing

  • 🏆 Win: Score all 16 cards before your 4th strike.
  • 💀 Lose: Accumulate 4 strikes.
